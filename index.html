Help Us Study Urban Birds

Join our community science project to monitor bird populations in Centrala

About Our Project

The Centrala Environmental Agency (CEA) is working with the Centrala Trust for Ornithology (CTO) to assist with urban planning. Regular exposure to nature, especially birds, can improve mental health and physical wellbeing of citizens.

We need your help to collect data about bird species in urban and suburban areas of Centrala. Your observations will help us make informed decisions about environmental planning.

How to Participate

  1. Register for an account on our website
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Record your bird observations in your area
  4. Upload photos of the birds you observe (optional)
  5. Share your observations on our message board

Your contributions will help us understand the distribution and behavior of bird species in Centrala.
